WELCOME

This is my personal site where I can freely express myself and experiment with designs alpong with storing my art. I'm also an artist who stores my art here on the internet, I'm primarily active on instagram, toyhouse, various discord servers and lastly here.

I created this website December 29. 2024 as a little project of mine as a way to experiment with html after getting irritated with sites like Carrd and Strawpage for their limited customization.
Despite me having this site for a while, I didn't really start to develop it until
March of 2025, which you can view in ARCHIVES.

You can find a LOT more in the ABOUT ME section. You can view more things about me personally (BORINGGG) or view my WIPs through a window! You can also view more graphics there along with external links and socials

SIDENOTE: I added my name to the tags to make it easier for to search for, I also hate the way the neocities search bar works when trying to search for a specific site. I'm not an egomaniac.

Design Choices

Contrary to how it may seem, my favorite color is NOT green, though it is one of them. My favorite colour is burgundy but, for what I'm trying to achieve, it doesn't fit.

Anyway, a lot of my design choices (with a few exceptions) come from my childhood memories of being on early 2010s internet, design wise.

Certian colors bring me a sense of nostalgia or comfort such as neon green, red and hot pink (with glitter specifically), you'll see some of that incorporated throughout the site..
